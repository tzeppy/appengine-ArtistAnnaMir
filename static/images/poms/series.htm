The pomegranate has been used throughout history as a symbol of humanity's most fundamental beliefs and desires, including life and death, rebirth and eternal life, fertility and marriage, abundance and prosperity.

Every aspect of the pomegranate has come to symbolize something ... its shape, color, seeds, juice.

In this series I saw pomegranate as the reason for the annual change of seasons (Persephone and Hades story), so the series is a meditation on winter, a subset of the series "Texture of Light".
